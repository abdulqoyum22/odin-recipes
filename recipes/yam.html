Yam And Egg

Description

Yam and egg is a popular and versatile Nigerian comfort food that pairs starchy, fork-tender yams with a flavorful egg-based sauce or stew.

It is widely enjoyed for breakfast, lunch, or dinner due to its ease of preparation and nutritious components.

Ingredients

  • Yam Tubers
  • Eggs
  • Tomatoes
  • Onions
  • Peppers (like Scotch bonnet for heat, bell peppers for flavor)
  • Cooked with vegetable oil
  • Seasonings such as
    • Thyme
    • Curry Powder
    • Seasoning Cubes (like Maggi/Knorr)
