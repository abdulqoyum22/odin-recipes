Jollof Rice And Chicken

Description

Jollof rice and chicken is a vibrant, one-pot West African dish featuring rice cooked in a rich, spicy tomato-pepper base with aromatic spices, absorbing flavors from seasoned chicken pieces cooked alongside or seperately.

It's known for ita signature reddish-orange hue, smoky-sweet flavor, and is a beloved celebratory meal, often served with fried plaintains and salad, embodying comfort food with deep cultural roots.

  • Chicken
  • Long-grain rice
  • Tomatoes (fresh/paste)
  • Onions
  • Peppers (bell/scotch bonnet)
  • Garlic
  • Ginger
  • Oil
  • Spices like
    • Curry Powder
    • Thyme
    • Stock Cubes
    • Salt
    • Pepper

Steps

Part 1: Prepare the Chicken and Stock

  1. Season the chicken
  2. Cook the chicken
  3. Fry the chicken

Part 2: Prepare the Jollof Base (Stew)

  1. Reduce the tomato mix
  2. Saute aromatics
  3. Fry the tomato paste and mix
  4. Combine and season the base
  5. Add stock

Part 3: Cook the Rice

  1. Wash the rice
  2. Combine and cook
  3. Cover tightly and simmer
  4. Fluff and serve
