Potato Latkes


Description

A potato latkes is a traditiona Jewish potato pancake or fritter, made from grated potatoes, onions, eggs, and a binder, which is then pan-fried until golden brown and crispy.

They are especially popular during a specific time and typically served with applesauce and sour cream.

Ingredients

  • Potatoes
  • Onion
  • Eggs
  • Flour (or matzo meal/starch)
  • Salt
  • Pepper
  • Oil
  1. Prepare the Potatoes and Onions
  2. Remove Excess Moisture
  3. Mix the Better
  4. Heat the Oil
  5. Fry the Latkes
  6. Cook Until Golden Brown
  7. Drain and Serve
